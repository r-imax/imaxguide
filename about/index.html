    About This Site

    Brief History

    A list was previously available on Wikipedia, but was removed by other editors in a policy disagreement. The last snapshot of that page is available here: List of IMAX Venues (as of May 5th 2024)

    If you'd like the XML Export, a copy from May 1st 2024 is available here: Wikipedia-20240501181526.xml.

    Why create this site?

    This site exists to provide a consolidated place to hold the lists, freely accessible, without ads. I wanted to make it more useful by adding sorting options, and more features will come in the future. There is also a Wiki planned for a future theatre directory, as well as including Xenon screens in a separate list.

    Where can I find the source files?

    The code and CommonMark files used to generate this site are available on GitHub: imaxguide. The information to build those files comes from several people, with an extra special thanks to u/NickLandis.

    How can I help?

    Suggestions and corrections are always accepted. You can reach me on Reddit (u/anthonylavado), Mastodon (@anthonylavado:social.bonifacelabs.ca), or by e-mail (updates [AT] 143190.xyz).

    If you have comments or concerns that are not related to updating information, please send those to admin [AT] 143190.xyz.
